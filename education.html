Sketches

When I was in school the only part of art I was ever half good at was sketching. I'd like to spend some time improving at some point, but it is very time consuming.

On the left is a sketch I did of the actress Lucy Liu last summer. Again I stuck to black and white rather than colour as it's a little bit easier!

In the past I tended to stick to pencil drawings of comic book characters as I liked the look of them and they were easier. I think that this was my first attempt at drawing a real person. It's still come out a little comic-esque but I don't think it's terrible for a first attempt.

Introduction

I've always quite enjoyed art and tried to make time for it when I can. I've never really studied it properly because I was always more interested in the sciences, however it would be nice to maybe do classes in the future.

Mainly I do black and white pen and ink drawings as they are less of a commitment than painting or anything similar to that. However I have dabbled in a couple of different things and you can look at them using the tabs on the left of the page.

Anyway, I hope you enjoy looking through some of my stuff!
